Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, and we do not access your bank accounts or financial institutions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not use your financial information for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical safeguards — including encrypted storage and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to render typography. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when you load the app. Beyond this, we do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently. You can contact us via the support link in the app or by emailing us directly.
